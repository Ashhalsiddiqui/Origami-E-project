Origami Spinning Toy Tutorial

Steps to Make an Spinning Toy

  1. Start with a square sheet of paper.
  2. Fold the paper in half diagonally both ways to create an "X" crease.
  3. Fold the paper in half vertically and horizontally to create a "+" crease.
  4. Collapse the paper into a triangle using the creases.
  5. Fold the bottom corners of the triangle up to the top point.
  6. Flip the model over and repeat the same folds on the other side.
  7. Fold the outer edges toward the center to form the spinner shape.
  8. Pinch the center slightly to loosen it for better spinning.
  9. Hold the edges lightly and blow on one side to make it spin!
